INTRODUCTION

Hangyodon, a unique and endearing from the world of Sanrio, is a lovable fish-like with a quirky personality.
Born on March 14th under the sign of Pisces, Hangyodon possesses a gentle and somewhat mysterious nature. His distinctive appearance,
characterized by a toothy grin, round glasses, and a perpetually bemused expression, adds to his charm and makes him instantly recognizable.
He's described as a little bit of a lonely romantic, who doesn't express a ton of emotion except when with his best friend, Sayuri, a cheerful octopus.

Despite his unconventional looks, Hangyodon's true appeal lies in his relatable and friendly demeanor. He's an aspiring comedian
with a passion for stand-up comedy, often dreaming of making people laugh and bringing joy to others. His hobbies include
collecting his own merchandise and indulging in his favorite foods, such as shrimp crackers, hot pot, and hot springs.

Hangyodon's unique blend of humor, sensitivity, and gentle spirit has made him a beloved character among Sanrio fans.
His ability to connect with others through laughter and understanding resonates with people of all ages, making him a
symbol of friendship and positivity in the Sanrio universe.

Hangyodon is an official character licensed by Sanrio.
